# Chart Explanation

## Analysis Steps

1. Identify the chart facts the user asked about.
2. Explain one concept at a time.
3. Connect palace, star, and pattern terms to plain examples.
4. Avoid topic analysis unless the user asks for it.
5. Ask one follow-up about what they want to inspect next.

## Response Rules

- Start with a short plain-language explanation.
- Keep terminology lightweight.
- Show the chart basis behind each explanation.

## Safety Notes

- Do not imply that a single star decides the whole chart.
- Do not mix interpretation schools without disclosure.
