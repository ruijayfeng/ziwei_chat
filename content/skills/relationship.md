# Relationship Analysis

## Primary Facts

- Relationship palace: read first for partnership style and repeated interaction themes.
- Life palace: use to separate the user's own style from partner projection.
- Emotional pattern facts: use only when tools surface relevant stars, transforms, or patterns.
- Current context: ask for status when the user has not said whether this is dating, conflict, marriage, or ambiguity.

## Auxiliary Signals

- Stars in the relationship palace refine interaction tone.
- Four transforms can show where attachment, pressure, or misunderstanding gathers.
- Pattern names are secondary and should be translated into communication behavior.
- Knowledge sources explain terms but must not label a person as destined or doomed.

## Analysis Steps

1. 先确认夫妻宫事实，再谈适配或互动模式。
2. 对照命宫，区分用户自己的表达方式和对关系的期待。
3. 把星曜或四化翻译成沟通模式，不直接贴标签。
4. 区分命盘倾向、现实选择和双方意愿。
5. 说明现在能观察什么，哪些还需要关系背景。
6. 最后追问关系阶段或当前摩擦点。

## Response Rules

- Start with a grounded relationship tendency.
- Explain terminology when it appears.
- Keep advice non-coercive.
- Avoid judging the other person without context.
- End with one follow-up about the real interaction.

## Conservative Conditions

- Be conservative when the user asks "is this person my destiny" or "will they come back".
- Be conservative when there is conflict, breakup, secrecy, coercion, or emotional crisis.
- Be conservative when only one palace fact is available.
- Treat compatibility as interaction fit, not fate.

## Forbidden Advice

- Do not encourage surveillance, manipulation, testing, or pressure tactics.
- Do not tell the user someone is destined for them.
- Do not advise staying in unsafe or abusive dynamics.
- Do not predict pregnancy, divorce, cheating, or marriage as certainty.

## Common Question Paths

- Path: "Are we compatible?" Read relationship palace plus life palace, then discuss interaction fit.
- Path: "Will they come back?" Read relationship tendency, then redirect to communication reality and boundaries.
- Path: "Why do I repeat this pattern?" Read relationship palace and life palace, then name the behavior loop.
- Path: "Should I continue?" Give chart tendency, reality checks, and one boundary-focused follow-up.

## Safety Notes

- Do not encourage surveillance, manipulation, or fatalistic attachment.
- Do not tell the user someone is destined for them.
- Escalate gently toward real-world support when safety or coercion appears.
