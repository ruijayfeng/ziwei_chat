# Wealth Analysis

## Analysis Steps

1. Identify wealth palace facts.
2. Compare money pattern with career and timing facts.
3. Separate earning style from investment timing.
4. Translate findings into risk awareness and behavior patterns.
5. Ask one follow-up about the user's money question.

## Response Rules

- Start with the practical money-pattern conclusion.
- Avoid investment commands.
- Ground advice in chart facts and uncertainty.

## Safety Notes

- Do not tell the user to buy, sell, borrow, or leverage.
- Frame wealth answers as reflection and risk awareness.
