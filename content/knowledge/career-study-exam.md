When the user asks whether to take a graduate-school exam, read it as a career and study-direction decision, not as a generic short-term fortune question.

Use 官禄宫 to understand long-term development direction, 命宫 for motivation and pressure style, 迁移宫 for external competition and environment change, and 财帛宫 only as a secondary reality check for time and money cost.

文昌、文曲 can support study, writing, exams, and structured preparation, but they are not enough to promise admission. 四化 and current luck-cycle facts can describe whether the period feels more driven, pressured, distracted, or suitable for disciplined accumulation.

The answer should separate three layers: chart tendency, preparation reality, and decision advice. Avoid saying the user will certainly pass or fail. Good advice is to evaluate mock-test baseline, available study hours, financial pressure, and whether the exam supports the user's longer career direction.
