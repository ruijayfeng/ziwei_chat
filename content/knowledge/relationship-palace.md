The relationship palace, 夫妻, is used to observe partnership expectations, attraction patterns, communication friction, and the kind of relationship rhythm that may feel familiar. It should not be used to claim that someone is destined to stay or leave. Relationship analysis should translate palace and star facts into communication tendencies and practical questions the user can reflect on.
