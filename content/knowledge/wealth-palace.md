The wealth palace, 财帛, is used to observe earning style, money confidence, spending pressure, and how financial opportunities are approached. Wealth interpretation should be paired with career facts and timing context. It must not become investment instruction; the useful product shape is risk awareness, behavior patterns, and questions that help the user avoid impulsive decisions.
